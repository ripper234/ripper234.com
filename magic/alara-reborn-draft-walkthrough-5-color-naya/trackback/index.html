Pick 14

* This walkthrough was created by Create Your Own Magic Draft Walkthrough

Only at the end of the draft I discover the that I’m in fact drafting Naya deck and not a Bant deck – the two Waveskimmer Avens gave me the (wrong) impression I was drafting Bant.
This is the most bomb-heavy deck I drafted in a while. Here is the decklist:

Creatures

  • Sanctum Gargoyle
  • Ranger of Eos (played one of the 2 I had)
  • Waveskimmer Aven
  • Qasali Ambusher
  • Knight of the Reliquary
  • Nactal Hunt-Pride
  • Cylian Sunsinger
  • Aven Trailblaze
  • Vagrant Plowbeast
  • Paragon of the Amesha
  • Tukatounge Thallid
  • Retalliator Griffin
  • Winged Coatl
  • Grizzled Leotau
  • Rhox Brute
  • Cerodon Yearling

Spells

  • Resounding Silence
  • Obelisk of Alara
  • Martial Coup
  • Sangrite Backlash

Lands & Mana

  • Armillary Sphere
  • Trace of Abundance X 2
  • Bant Panorama
  • Rupture Spire
  • Island
  • Mount X 2
  • Forest X 4
  • Plains X 4

I went 2-0 against a Bant deck which was similar in spirit to my deck, minus all the bombs. Game one my opponent gained a huge amount of life with an exalted Rhox Monk, but was stopped by activated Paragon of Amesha + Nacatl Hunt-Pride which gained me an even greater amount of life while killing his dudes one by one.
I finished that game with a 8 lands and a ridiculous hand of Obelisk of Alara, Martial Coup & 2 Waveskimmer Avens.

Game 2 was even easier. An amusing moment was when I discovered he had a Hunt-Pride of his own, and the next turn I draw & cast my Hunt-Pride.
